# 🌫 Assumption: Acredemia has a product-native referral currency

## The assumption
There exists some in-product good (faster verification, premium profile visibility, priority recruiter access…) valuable enough to serve as a Dropbox-style referral reward.

## Why we currently believe it
Unverified — extrapolated from [[T-two-sided-incentives-drive-referral-virality]]. **This is the exact point the theory's boundary condition warns about.**

## What would validate / invalidate it
- Validate: a small survey/experiment shows students act on a non-cash in-product reward.
- Invalidate: only cash moves the needle → the two-sided-*product-native* theory doesn't transfer here.
