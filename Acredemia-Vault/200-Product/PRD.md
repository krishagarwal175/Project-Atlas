# PRD (living)

> Requirements evolve here; each material change → [[Changelog]] entry.

## Primary user
Founder-Operator (internal Acredemia). Secondary: co-founder / future-self.

## Core jobs-to-be-done
1. Capture knowledge & observations with near-zero friction.
2. Keep strategic **questions** alive and accumulate evidence around them.
3. Turn case studies into **patterns** → **theories** that survive contradiction.
4. Make a decision with visible evidence + explicit assumptions + a **quality score** separate from outcome.
5. Attach a falsifiable **experiment**; force a retrospective; feed the lesson back.
6. See **what changed** in the market and whether it touches our assumptions.
7. Trust nothing forever — **freshness/decay** and **confidence** are always visible.

## Hard constraints
Open-source only · no paid APIs · no cloud LLM in production (Ollama local optional) · vault is source of truth · every output explainable.

## Success = daily unforced use
The real test: does the founder open this for a decision *unprompted*? Timestamps make it observable for free.
