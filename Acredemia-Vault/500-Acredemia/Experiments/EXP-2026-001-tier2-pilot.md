# 🧪 Tier-2 college verification pilot

## Hypothesis
If we onboard 2–3 Tier-2 colleges, then ≥30% of pilot students complete verification **and** ≥3 recruiters express paid interest within 6 weeks.

## Success metric
- % students completing verification; count of recruiters expressing willingness-to-pay.

## Leading indicator (early signal)
- Week-2 student sign-up rate from a single college.

## Failure condition (kills it early)
- < 10% student completion by week 3, or zero recruiter interest by week 4.

## MoSCoW scope
- **Must:** verification flow + 1 college. **Should:** recruiter outreach. **Could:** referral mechanic. **Won't:** payments integration.

## Expected ROI / rationale
Cheaply tests the core thesis of [[DEC-2026-001-tier2-beachhead]] before committing GTM budget.
