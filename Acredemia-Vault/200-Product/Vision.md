# Product Vision — Acredemia Research OS

**A founder's second brain that turns knowledge into understanding, understanding into tested theories, and theories into high-quality decisions — and remembers why.**

The Decision Engine is one subsystem of many. The center of gravity is the compounding loop:

`Question → Evidence → Understanding → Theory (survives contradiction) → Decision (quality-scored) → Experiment → Outcome → back into Evidence`

## Three principles (priority order)
1. **Knowledge compounds or it's worthless** — no orphan notes; everything links into the graph.
2. **Provenance over prediction** — never forecasts; shows what's known, from where, how strongly, and what would change our mind.
3. **Friction is the enemy** — capture is near-zero-friction (plain markdown); the compute layer does the organizing.

## Non-goal
Never claims to predict the future. It makes reasoning explicit, comparable, and revisitable.
