# 🔁 Leakage + discount-demand kills marketplaces

## The regularity
Marketplaces that (a) acquire demand via discounts and (b) provide little ongoing value after the first match tend to collapse — users disloyal, transactions leak off-platform.

## Supporting cases
- [[HomeJoy-marketplace-failure]] (platform leakage — customers/cleaners went direct)
- [[Groupon-discount-growth]] (discount-acquired demand didn't retain)

> Second case surfaced by the Synthesis engine as a NEW {Groupon, Homejoy} cluster and added here — the drafted-understanding loop working end to end.

## Does this rise to a Theory?
Plausibly, causal. Both share **discount/incentive-acquired demand fails to retain**; Homejoy adds the **leakage** mechanism. Before promoting to a theory, run a contradiction search (find discount-driven marketplaces that *did* retain) and tighten the boundary conditions.
