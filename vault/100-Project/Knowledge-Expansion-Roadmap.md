# 🗺 Knowledge Expansion Roadmap

> The vault is a **curated research library**, not an encyclopedia. This roadmap sequences the *next* highest-leverage notes so growth stays intentional. Rule: a note is added only when it will improve a future decision. Quality over quantity, always.

## Generation 1 — DONE (2026-07-10)
The reasoning + mechanics foundation: 12 decision-science mental models, 8 frameworks, product/growth/finance mechanics, research methods, and Acredemia's constitution (personas, competitors, marketplace dynamics, long-term vision). These are the **textbooks** — foundational at 100k notes.

## Generation 2 — next (highest leverage first)
1. **Company histories, sourced** (Startup-History): Stripe (DX), Airbnb (photography experiment / near-death), Amazon (flywheel/Prime), a bootstrapped Indian trio (Zerodha, Zoho, Razorpay). *Rule: every metric cited, else `[unverified]`.*
2. **Finance depth:** fundraising & dilution, valuation basics, revenue models, marketplace take-rate.
3. **Decision science:** game theory basics, counterfactual thinking, OODA loop, Cynefin.
4. **Product depth:** retention/activation, discovery vs. delivery, feature-creep, roadmapping.
5. **Marketing depth:** PLG, pricing strategy, distribution/virality, community.
6. **Software (reference):** knowledge graphs, information retrieval / vector search, event-driven & DDD, local-first — some already covered by [[System-Architecture]] / ADRs; add only the reusable-theory notes.

## Generation 3 — as needed
Additional frameworks (Blue Ocean, Ansoff, Double Diamond, PESTLE), more case studies **driven by real questions** — write the case study when a live decision needs the precedent, not to fill folders.

## Governance
Every new reference note: proper frontmatter, ≥2 cross-links, tagged, sourced. The [[Vault-Governance|governance bot]] flags orphans/untagged/stale. See [[Research-Backlog]] for the open-question-driven queue.
