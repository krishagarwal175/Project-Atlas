# 🧬 Mission

> **DRAFT for founder review.** Written from the project context (end-to-end verified hiring ecosystem). Edit to your exact conviction — this anchors the whole multi-scale spine and is consulted by the Decision Engine on every recommendation.

## Mission (one sentence)
Make early-career hiring trustworthy by making every credential, skill, and experience claim independently verifiable — so a candidate's record speaks for itself and a recruiter can trust it without a background-check tax.

## Vision (10-year)
A world where "verified on Acredemia" is the default proof of a candidate's record across campuses and employers — the trust layer between students and the people who hire them.

## Who we serve, in order
1. **Students / early-career candidates** — the supply of verified records; the flywheel starts here.
2. **Recruiters / employers** — the demand that monetizes trust.
3. **Colleges / institutions** — distribution and a source of authoritative verification.

## What we will NOT become
- A paywalled gate that makes students pay to prove who they are.
- A resume-embellishment tool that inflates rather than verifies.
- A data broker that sells student data to the highest bidder.
