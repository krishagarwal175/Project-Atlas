# 🧬 Founder Beliefs

> **DRAFT for founder review.** Strong opinions that shape strategy, explicitly labelled as *beliefs* (not proven) so the Contradiction Engine can challenge them over time.

- **B1 — Trust, not features, is the moat.** Anyone can build a profile app; few can make recruiters *trust* it. — confidence: emerging — challenged by: a competitor winning on features despite weaker verification.
- **B2 — The student side must be free to seed the network.** — confidence: speculative — challenged by: evidence that students will pay, or that recruiters fund the whole model without a large free student base. Tied to [[Q-should-verification-stay-free]].
- **B3 — Verification value is highest where credibility gaps are widest** (e.g. Tier-2 vs Tier-1). — confidence: speculative — challenged by: [[EXP-2026-001-tier2-pilot]] failing. Tied to [[Q-tier2-before-tier1]].
- **B4 — Supply (verified students) is harder to build than demand (recruiters).** — confidence: speculative — challenged by: recruiters proving to be the true cold-start bottleneck. Tied to [[Q-recruiters-first-or-students-first]].

> When a belief accumulates contradicting evidence in its ledger, the Governance Bot surfaces it for a [[TPL-review|belief review]].
