# 🧬 Values

> **DRAFT for founder review.** Each value has a *behavioral* definition and a stated tension — poster words are useless.

1. **Verifiable over impressive** — In practice: we ship the boring feature that proves a claim before the flashy one that markets it. In tension with: growth speed and demo sizzle.
2. **Student trust is the asset** — In practice: no decision that erodes student trust survives, even if it lifts short-term revenue. In tension with: monetization pressure.
3. **Evidence over intuition (but honest about both)** — In practice: we write the assumption down and attach a cheap experiment before a big bet. In tension with: founder conviction and speed.
4. **Reversible bets first** — In practice: prefer a pilot we can unwind over an irreversible commitment. In tension with: bold all-in moves.
5. **Own our data and reasoning** — In practice: knowledge lives in portable files we control, not a vendor's black box. In tension with: convenience of managed SaaS.
