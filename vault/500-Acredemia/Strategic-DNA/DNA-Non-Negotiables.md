# 🧬 Non-Negotiables (the hard guardrail)

> **DRAFT for founder review.** Lines Acredemia will not cross **even if crossing them maximizes growth.** The Decision Engine flags any recommendation violating one as `⚠ DNA-conflict` — it cannot be silently ranked #1; the founder must consciously override.

- **NN1 — Never fake, inflate, or "assume" a verification.** A verified claim is verified against a real source, or it is not verified. Trust is the entire product.
- **NN2 — Never sell or broker student personal data.** Data is used to serve the student and the hiring match, nothing else.
- **NN3 — Never paywall a student's ability to prove who they are.** Students may pay for *convenience/premium*, never for *basic truth about themselves*.
- **NN4 — Never let a paying recruiter alter or suppress a candidate's true record.** The record is the candidate's, not the buyer's.
- **NN5 — No dark patterns to inflate engagement or verification counts.** Metrics we report to ourselves must be honest or the whole epistemic system is poisoned.

## How this is enforced
Every [[TPL-decision|Decision]] has a "Strategic DNA check" section. A recommendation violating a Non-Negotiable is surfaced, not suppressed.
