# 🔁 Land in one unit, then expand

## The regularity
Successful bottom-up products deliver value inside a **single small unit** (a team, a cohort) before any org-wide or network-wide commitment, then expand user-driven.

## Supporting cases
- [[Slack-bottom-up]] (team)
- [[Figma-community]] (collaboration unit)

## Does this rise to a Theory?
Candidate. Would need a causal claim + a contradiction search (find bottom-up products that failed *because* they had no valuable small unit).
