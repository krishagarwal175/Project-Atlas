# 🔁 Two-sided, product-native incentive loops

## The regularity
Across successful referral programs, the reward tends to be (a) **two-sided** and (b) paid in the **product's own currency** rather than cash.

## Supporting cases
- [[Dropbox-referral]] (product-native reward)
- [[PayPal-referral]] (cash reward — same two-sided structure)
