# 🌅 Acredemia — Long-Term Vision

> Where this goes if it works. Draft for founder review — the 10-year picture that today's decisions ladder toward. Anchors the multi-scale spine below [[DNA-Mission]].

## The 10-year picture
"Verified on Acredemia" becomes the **default trust layer** between early-career candidates and the people who hire them — starting with Indian campuses, expanding along the graph of colleges and employers. A candidate owns a portable, verified record of who they are and what they've done; a recruiter trusts it without a background-check tax.

## The compounding assets (moats)
1. **Trust** — the hardest thing to build, the easiest to lose; our non-negotiable ([[DNA-Non-Negotiables]]).
2. **Two-sided network effects** ([[MM-network-effects]]) — verified supply ↔ recruiter demand.
3. **Owned verified history** — switching costs; the record lives with the student.

## Candidate North Star ([[F-okrs-north-star]])
**Verified candidates whose profile a recruiter actively engages with, weekly** — reflects both sides and predicts revenue.

## What we refuse to become (anti-vision)
A paywalled gate on truth, a résumé-inflation tool, or a data broker ([[DNA-Non-Negotiables]]). Growth never overrides these.

## How we get there (sequence)
Beachhead density ([[Q-tier2-before-tier1]]) → prove recruiter WTP ([[Q-recruiters-first-or-students-first]]) → ignite the loop ([[Marketplace-Dynamics]]) → expand along the college/employer graph.
